We Supply Kenya Essential Oils

Cypress

Cypress oil has a woody fresh scent which is the most masculine aromas. It is used for urinary and liver treatment and usually blends well with many other essential oil like citrus oils.

Eucalyptus

Eucalyptus oil has a clear, fresh and sharp and uniquely exquisite scent. It is useful as warming oil when applied for muscular aches and pains, sprains, poor blood circulation, rheumatoid and arthritis.

Rosemary

Rosemary oil has a camphorous, herbal smell. It is effective for massage, general respiratory issues, and is a skin and hair booster. Also very stimulating and blends well with other oils.

Frankincence

Frankincense has a woody, spicy, haunting smell, a little bit camphoric, but very pleasant. It can calm, soothe the whole body and mind. It also refreshes the mind, slowing down and deepening breath and it comes in very handy when meditating.

Leleshwa

Leleshwa is among popular Kenya essential oils from dark-olive, green-leaved shrub. Crushed leaves usually have a very strong camphoraceous smell. It acts as a natural preservative when added to body and skin care formulations.

Tea tree oil

Tea tree oil has light spicy ,quite pungent smell and is very pale in color with viscosity like water .It is a very powerful immune stimulant which increases the body’s ability to fight infections.

Neem oil

Neem oil is obtained from seed of tree which is scientifically known as Azadirachta indica. It is an oil which has a strange smell that can be masked by adding more fragrant essential oils and is used for therapeutic purposes.

Aloe gel

Commonly known as Aloe vera, it produces two substances –Latex and gel used  for medicines. Aloe gel is a clear-jelly like substance found in the inner part of the aloe plant leaf. It comes just beneath the plant’s skin and its color is yellowish.
